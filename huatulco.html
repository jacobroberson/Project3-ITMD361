Huatulco, Mexico

Airport: Aeropuerto Internacional de Bahías de Huatulco (HUX)

Santa María Huatulco is a town and municipality in Mexico. It lies within the state of Oaxaca on the south-western side of Mexico. Huatulco gets its name from an ancient legend and translates to "where they worship the tree." Hernán Cortés conquered the town of Huatulco on January 8th 1539. Currently, Huatulco has a population of about 50,000 and is known for its tourism.
